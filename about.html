What makes Us Great

RV Matrix tied up with a leading Industrial association in south India in 2010 and had implemented the ERP software in over 250 small and large-scale industries in southern India.

RV Matrix setup a centre of excellence in 2009 and started working on key automation projects in the Banking and Financial Services and Insurance (BFSI) space. This eventually led to offering services to a global leader in consulting, technology and outsourcing services with presence in over 40 countries. The RV team has pioneered some of the key automation projects in this organization.
